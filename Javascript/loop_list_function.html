Fittingly, it is also home to one of the world's most futuristi c footballers. Versatile, lightning-quick, two-footed and lethal in front of g oal, Heung-Min Son is the paradigm of the 21st-century player. A footballer so industrious, so efficient and so finely tuned that you could be forgiven for wondering if he had been machine-engineered. There is no superfluity with Son—no pointless tricks, no showy to uches, no playing to the gallery. He has only one trick—the stepover—and he only ever deploys it to burst past befuddled defenders on his way to goal. The 26-year-old South Korean plays as if he only ever has five seconds to influence the game, which has helped turn him into one of the most dangerous attacking players in Europe.
